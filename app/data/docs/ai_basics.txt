Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn.

There are three main types of AI:
1. Narrow AI – designed for specific tasks (e.g., chatbots, recommendation systems)
2. General AI – human-level intelligence (still theoretical)
3. Super AI – beyond human intelligence (future concept)

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data without explicit programming.

Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks with multiple layers.

Key applications of AI include:
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Computer Vision
- Robotics
- Recommendation systems

Popular AI frameworks include TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-learn.
